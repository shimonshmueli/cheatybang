About & Privacy

A community reference for Claude Code.

What this is

CheatyBang is a static cheat sheet for Claude Code. It runs entirely in your browser. There's no backend, no analytics, no tracking, no account.

What we collect

Nothing. Your settings (theme, scheme, density, environment, level, pinned items) are stored in your browser's localStorage and never leave your device.

Third-party assets

This page loads CSS / JS from public CDNs (Tailwind Play, Alpine.js, Fuse.js, Prism.js) and fonts from Google Fonts. Those services may log basic request metadata under their own privacy policies. No data we control is sent to them.
